Creative Projects

Everyone talks about creativity - but why is it actually important?

But creativity is not just about self-realization. It is precisely the community, in a creative environment, that offers a rich breeding ground for ideas to grow and create something new. Instead of orienting ourselves to fixed patterns and behaviors, our creativity enables us to question the supposed ideals of our time of beautiful and ugly or good and bad. A creative environment motivates us to break out of this thinking. Thus, there is a lot of freedom in being creative. And: creativity offers room for the imperfect.
